About Routes with Friends

Trip Planning Made Easy

Trying to figure out the best travel plan for your group trip? Routes with Friends maps an optimal travel plan based on distance and user-input locations.

Unique Collaborative System

Allows users to collaborate, edit, and give feedback with immediate application response in real-time, allowing for hassle-free planning.

Simple and Functional Design

Our clean and organized design makes Routes with Friends easy and straightforward to use. Add locations and view the plan with ease.
